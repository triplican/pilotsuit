The Pilot Suit

A children's picture book for kids aged 4 to 8 years old

In this charming and thought-provoking children's picture book, a little boy dreams of becoming a pilot. This dream, coupled with some chance encounters, take the little one on a journey to distant lands that brings him new friends, teaches him important life lessons and ultimately shapes his very destiny.
